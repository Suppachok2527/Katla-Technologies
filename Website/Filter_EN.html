Overview of worldwide microplastic pollution

You can see worldwide measurements regarding the amount of plastic / km².

In general, the measurement situation is far from being nationwide. So far, the typical shipping routes and plastic hotspots have been analyzed.
The black fields on the map represent collection points by ocean currents for plastic parts and microplastics.

The high concentrations at these hotspots and collection points are clearly visible. Otherwise only on the usual ones Shipping routes were measured and plastic or microplastics could be found almost everywhere, one can assume that Plastic, as well as microplastics, are an all-encompassing problem.
